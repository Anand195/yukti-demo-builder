# Business Scenarios Reference

For each business type: Tab 2 recovery scenario, Tab 3 pipeline scenario, typical qualification questions, CRM fields, and pain points.

---

## 1. Real Estate (Residential)

**Primary pain**: No-shows kill weekend site visit plans. Team spends 3–4 hrs/day on tele-calling to qualify leads already in CRM.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *No-Show Recovery*. Saturday visit confirmed → lead doesn't show → ScaleYukti sends "Are you on your way?" message → if no response → voice call → if interested → re-book for Sunday slot from standby list.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Weekend pipeline planner. 3-tier slot system (Confirmed Primary / Warm Standby / On-Call Reserve). Working backwards from 3–4 closings/month → 20–25 visits → 14 weekend confirmed bookings → 65 pipeline leads.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "What's your budget range for a home? (e.g., 40L–60L, 60L–80L, 80L+)"
2. "Which area/location are you looking in?"
3. "How many BHK do you need? (2BHK / 3BHK / 4BHK)"
4. "Are you looking for ready-to-move or under-construction?"
5. "Is this for self-use or investment?"

**CRM fields**: Lead Name, Phone, Source, Budget, BHK, Preferred Location, Community/Caste, Possession Timeline, Matched Projects, Site Visit Date, Lead Score, Status, Assigned To

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Chatbot + Broadcasting (2000+ database) + Automation + Drip Campaigns

## 2. Clinic / Hospital / Healthcare

**Primary pain**: Manual appointment booking takes 10–15 min per patient. Reminders are done by calling one by one. 25–35% no-show rate.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Appointment No-Show Recovery*. Appointment confirmed for 11 AM → patient doesn't show → ScaleYukti sends "Your appointment was at 11 AM — shall we reschedule?" with quick-reply buttons → patient taps "Yes, reschedule" → next available slot offered instantly.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Daily appointment planner. Slots per doctor per day. Target: 80%+ utilization. No-show buffer = 20% extra bookings.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "Which doctor/specialist would you like to see?"
2. "What's the reason for your visit? (Consultation / Follow-up / Test results / Emergency)"
3. "Do you have insurance? If yes, which provider?"
4. "Preferred date and time for appointment?"
5. "Is this for you or a family member? Age?"

**CRM fields**: Patient Name, Phone, Doctor Preferred, Appointment Type, Insurance Provider, Preferred Date/Time, Symptoms/Reason, Previous Visits, Appointment Status, Reminder Sent, Source (Referral/Ad/Walk-in)

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Automation (instant booking confirmation) + Voice Calling Agent (reminders) + Chatbot + Drip Campaigns (follow-up care)

## 3. Salon / Spa / Beauty Studio

**Primary pain**: Bookings come through calls/Instagram DMs — hard to track. 20–30% cancellations happen last minute with no time to fill the slot.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Cancellation Recovery*. Booking confirmed for 3 PM → cancellation or no-show → ScaleYukti instantly messages the next person on the waitlist → slot filled within minutes.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Weekly appointment pipeline. Stylists per day × slots per stylist. Revenue per slot. Target utilization 85%+.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "Which service are you looking for? (Haircut / Color / Keratin / Facial / Bridal / etc.)"
2. "Do you have a preferred stylist?"
3. "What's your preferred date and time?"
4. "Is this for a special occasion?"
5. "Have you visited us before?"

**CRM fields**: Client Name, Phone, Service Type, Preferred Stylist, Appointment Date/Time, Special Occasion, Last Visit Date, Loyalty Points, Booking Source, Status

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Automation (instant booking confirmation + reminder) + Drip Campaigns (re-engage lapsed clients every 45 days) + Broadcasting (seasonal offers)

## 4. Restaurant / Café / Cloud Kitchen

**Primary pain**: Table reservations come in through calls — staff busy during peak hours. Online orders have high abandonment. Repeat customer retention is low.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Reservation No-Show Recovery*. Table booked for 8 PM → 30 min before, no confirmation → ScaleYukti sends "Your table is ready! Confirm arrival or need to reschedule?" → Quick reply buttons → instant response.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Weekend reservation planner. Tables × turns per evening. Target: 90%+ occupancy on Friday/Saturday.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "How many guests? (2 / 4 / 6 / large group)"
2. "Preferred date and time?"
3. "Any dietary preferences? (Veg / Non-Veg / Jain / Vegan)"
4. "Special occasion? (Birthday / Anniversary / Business)"
5. "Indoor or outdoor seating preference?"

**CRM fields**: Guest Name, Phone, Party Size, Reservation Date/Time, Dietary Preference, Occasion, Seating Preference, Reservation Status, Reminder Sent, Loyalty Visits

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Automation (reservation confirmation) + Broadcasting (weekly specials, new menu launch) + Drip Campaigns (bring back lapsed customers)

## 5. E-commerce / D2C / Retail

**Primary pain**: 70%+ cart abandonment. New product launches reach only 20% of customer list via email. Customer LTV is low because repeat purchase rate drops after month 3.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Cart Abandonment Recovery*. Customer adds to cart → doesn't checkout → 30 min later: "You left something behind!" message with product image + 1-tap checkout link → if no response → day 2 reminder with small offer.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Weekly sales pipeline. Visitors → add to cart → checkout → recovery campaigns → actual orders. Target: reduce cart abandonment from 70% to 45%.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "What are you shopping for today? (Category selection)"
2. "What's your budget range?"
3. "Is this a gift or for yourself?"
4. "What size / variant do you need?"
5. "Would you like to see more options in this range?"

**CRM fields**: Customer Name, Phone, Last Order Date, Total Orders, Cart Value, Product Interest, Preferred Category, Coupon Used, Abandonment Count, Loyalty Tier, Last WhatsApp Interaction

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Broadcasting (product launches, flash sales) + Automation (cart abandonment + order updates) + Drip Campaigns (retention sequences)

## 6. Education / Coaching / Ed-Tech

**Primary pain**: Demo class no-shows (40–50% of scheduled). Counselors spend 2–3 hrs/day calling leads manually. Leads go cold after the first call if not followed up within 24 hrs.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Demo Class No-Show Recovery*. Demo class scheduled for Saturday 11 AM → student doesn't join → ScaleYukti sends "You missed today's demo — here's the recording + 3 key takeaways" → day 2: "Want to book your personal counseling session?" → warm re-engage.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Monthly enrollment pipeline. Leads → demo class scheduled → attended → counseling → enrolled. Target: 15–20% lead-to-enrollment conversion.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "Which course are you interested in? (Course selection)"
2. "What's your current qualification/background?"
3. "What's your target (exam score / career goal / skill level)?"
4. "When are you planning to start? (Immediately / 1–3 months / 6 months)"
5. "What's your budget range for the course?"

**CRM fields**: Student Name, Phone, Course Interest, Current Qualification, Target Goal, Start Timeline, Budget, Demo Class Date, Attended (Y/N), Counseling Done, Enrollment Status, Source

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Automation (instant demo class confirmation) + Chatbot (qualification before counseling call) + Drip Campaigns (nurture non-enrollers) + Voice Calling Agent (no-show follow-up)

## 7. B2B / SaaS / Professional Services

**Primary pain**: Inbound demo requests go unanswered for 4–8 hrs. Follow-up after a demo depends entirely on the salesperson's memory. Long sales cycles mean leads go cold between touchpoints.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Ghosted Lead Recovery*. Demo done 5 days ago → no response to follow-up emails → ScaleYukti sends "Quick question — what did you think of the demo? Any blockers we can help with?" → re-opens the conversation without pressure.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Monthly deal pipeline. MQLs → demo scheduled → demo done → proposal sent → negotiation → closed. Target: 18–22% demo-to-close rate.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "What's the size of your team / company? (1–10 / 10–50 / 50–200 / 200+)"
2. "Which department/function will use this? (Sales / Marketing / Operations / Finance)"
3. "What's the main problem you're trying to solve?"
4. "Do you have an existing solution you're replacing?"
5. "What's your decision timeline? (Immediately / This quarter / Next quarter)"

**CRM fields**: Contact Name, Phone, Company, Designation, Team Size, Current Solution, Use Case, Decision Timeline, Budget, Demo Date, Proposal Sent, Deal Stage, Assigned AE

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Automation (instant demo confirmation) + Chatbot (pre-demo qualification) + Drip Campaigns (multi-touch nurture) + Voice Calling Agent (re-engagement)

## 8. Automobile Dealership

**Primary pain**: Test drive bookings are done manually over phone. 30–40% of enquiries never get a follow-up call because the team is busy. Database of past enquirers (who didn't buy) is never re-activated.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Test Drive No-Show Recovery*. Test drive confirmed for Saturday 11 AM → customer doesn't show → ScaleYukti sends "We've kept the [Model] ready for you — shall we reschedule your test drive?" with quick-reply options.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Weekly test drive pipeline. Enquiries → qualified → test drive booked → completed → offer sent → booking. Target: 30%+ enquiry-to-test-drive rate.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "Which model / variant are you interested in?"
2. "Is this for personal use or business?"
3. "Are you looking for a specific color or feature?"
4. "Will you be trading in a current vehicle?"
5. "What's your preferred finance/payment approach? (Cash / Loan / Lease)"

**CRM fields**: Customer Name, Phone, Model Interest, Variant, Color Preference, Trade-In (Y/N), Finance Type, Test Drive Date, Test Drive Done, Offer Sent, Booking Status, Source

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Automation (instant enquiry response) + Chatbot (model selection qualification) + Drip Campaigns (follow-up sequence post-test-drive) + Broadcasting (new model launches)

## 9. Wedding / Events / Banquet Hall

**Primary pain**: Venue enquiries come from multiple channels (calls, website, Instagram) and are hard to track. Follow-up is inconsistent. Seasonal demand means missed enquiries in peak wedding season = missed revenue.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Enquiry Follow-up Recovery*. Venue inquiry received → no site visit booked after 5 days → ScaleYukti sends "We have a few dates still available in [month] — shall we schedule a quick venue tour?" with a calendar link.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Monthly booking pipeline. Enquiries → site visit → proposal sent → booking confirmed. Target: 25–30% enquiry-to-booking rate.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "What type of event? (Wedding / Reception / Engagement / Corporate / Birthday)"
2. "How many guests are you expecting?"
3. "Preferred date or month?"
4. "Do you need in-house catering?"
5. "What's your total budget for the venue?"

**CRM fields**: Contact Name, Phone, Event Type, Guest Count, Preferred Date, Catering Required, Budget, Venue Tour Scheduled, Tour Done, Proposal Sent, Booking Status, Source

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Automation (instant inquiry acknowledgment) + Drip Campaigns (seasonal nurture) + Chatbot (qualification) + Broadcasting (availability + packages)

## 10. Travel / Tour Operator / Hotel

**Primary pain**: Package inquiries come in but follow-up is slow. Seasonal demand spikes are hard to handle manually. Repeat customers are never re-engaged with new packages.

**Tab 2 — Recovery**: *Inquiry Recovery*. Package inquiry submitted → 3 days, no booking → ScaleYukti sends "Prices for [destination] are going fast — here's what's still available this [month]" with urgency + quick-reply to book.

**Tab 3 — Pipeline**: Monthly booking pipeline. Inquiries → itinerary shared → follow-up → booking confirmed. Target: 20–25% inquiry-to-booking rate.

**Qualification questions**:
1. "Which destination are you interested in?"
2. "How many travelers? (Solo / Couple / Family / Group)"
3. "Preferred travel dates?"
4. "What's your per-person budget?"
5. "Any special requirements? (Honeymoon / Adventure / Pilgrimage / etc.)"

**CRM fields**: Traveler Name, Phone, Destination, Group Size, Travel Dates, Budget, Special Requirements, Itinerary Sent, Booking Status, Payment Status, Source

**Key ScaleYukti services**: Chatbot (package qualification) + Drip Campaigns (nurture + urgency sequences) + Broadcasting (seasonal deals, new packages) + Automation (booking confirmation)
