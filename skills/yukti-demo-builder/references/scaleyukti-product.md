# ScaleYukti — Product Reference

ScaleYukti is a WhatsApp Customer Engagement Platform. It connects to any WhatsApp Business API provider (EasySocial, Wati, AiSensy, Interakt, etc.) and adds five service layers on top.

---

## The 5 Services

### 1. Automation
**What it does**: Triggers pre-built WhatsApp message sequences automatically when a lead takes an action — fills a form, clicks a Meta Ad, visits a page, or reaches a CRM stage.

**Business value**: Every new lead gets an instant response (within 60 seconds) — no manual "hi, how can I help?" required. The team's first touchpoint is already done before anyone picks up the phone.

**Best for**: Any business getting inbound leads from Meta Ads, Google Ads, websites, or walk-in registration kiosks.

---

### 2. Chatbot (WhatsApp AI Chatbot)
**What it does**: Conducts a structured qualification conversation with the lead on WhatsApp. Asks 4–6 pre-defined questions, captures answers, scores the lead (HOT / WARM / COOL), and pushes the data to the CRM.

**Business value**: Replaces 15–20 minutes of manual tele-calling per lead with a 5-minute self-service chat. Team only calls leads that are already qualified. Rutvik's Excel sheet gets auto-filled.

**Best for**: Real estate (budget/BHK/location), clinics (symptoms/insurance/availability), education (course/budget/timeline), salons (service/stylist preference/date), B2B (company size/budget/timeline).

---

### 3. Drip Campaigns
**What it does**: Sends a scheduled series of WhatsApp messages over days or weeks to nurture leads who aren't ready yet. Each message is personalized (name, interest, last interaction).

**Business value**: WARM leads (45% of pipeline) get touched every 2–3 days without any manual effort. Keeps the business top-of-mind until the lead is ready to buy/book.

**Best for**: High-consideration purchases (real estate, luxury goods, education courses), long sales cycles (B2B), or seasonal businesses (wedding venues, travel).

---

### 4. Broadcasting
**What it does**: Sends a single personalized WhatsApp message to a segmented list of contacts simultaneously. Supports text, images, PDFs, videos, and buttons.

**Business value**: Re-activates the dormant database. A real estate company with 2,000+ old leads can send a targeted "new project launch" broadcast and generate fresh site visits from contacts who were cold for months.

**Best for**: Database revival, product launches, offer announcements, event invitations, seasonal promotions.

---

### 5. Voice Calling Agent
**What it does**: An AI voice agent that calls leads automatically, delivers a short script (new project / appointment reminder / offer announcement), and logs the result.

**Business value**: Reaches contacts who don't respond to WhatsApp messages. Handles 100 calls in the time a human makes 10.

**Best for**: No-show follow-up, reactivation campaigns, appointment reminders for clinic/salon, B2B prospecting at scale.

---

## How Services Map to Business Types

| Business Type | Primary Service | Secondary Services |
|---|---|---|
| Real Estate | Chatbot (qualification) + Broadcasting (database revival) | Automation, Drip Campaigns |
| Clinic / Hospital | Chatbot (appointment booking) + Voice Calling Agent (reminders) | Automation, Drip Campaigns |
| Salon / Spa | Automation (instant booking) + Chatbot (service selection) | Drip Campaigns, Broadcasting |
| Restaurant | Automation (reservation confirmation) + Broadcasting (offers) | Chatbot, Drip Campaigns |
| E-commerce / Retail | Broadcasting (abandoned cart) + Automation (order updates) | Chatbot, Drip Campaigns |
| Education / Coaching | Chatbot (demo class qualification) + Drip Campaigns (nurture) | Automation, Broadcasting |
| B2B / SaaS | Chatbot (demo request qualification) + Drip Campaigns (follow-up) | Automation, Voice Calling Agent |
| Automobile Dealership | Chatbot (test drive booking) + Voice Calling Agent (follow-up) | Automation, Drip Campaigns |
| Wedding / Events | Chatbot (inquiry qualification) + Drip Campaigns (nurture) | Broadcasting, Automation |
| Travel / Hospitality | Chatbot (package inquiry) + Broadcasting (seasonal offers) | Automation, Drip Campaigns |

| Lead response time | 2–6 hours (manual call) | < 60 seconds (automated) |

| No-show / cancellation rate | 40–50% | 8–15% |
| CRM fill rate | 60–70% (manual, incomplete) | 98%+ (automated, complete) |
| Team hours saved per person/day | — | 3–4 hours |
| Database revival rate | 0% (contacts sitting idle) | 3–8% response rate from broadcasts |
| Lead-to-visit/appointment conversion | 12–18% | 28–35% |
